About

Sloan Pardo

I am a 5th year senior in Mizzou’s Engineering school. Prior to college I graduated from an all-girls high school in St. Louis, St. Josephs Academy. This is where my interest in technology blossomed. I began my first web design course as a junior in 2016. Moving on to college, it is important for me to be involved in art. Leading me to choose Digital Story Telling as my major. After starting classes I realize that something is missing and that I am not happy with my decision. I then take a leap of faith, deciding to change my major. I want a challenge that I can also make a career out of. Today, my major is Information Technology. I have a certification in cybersecurity as well as a minor in art. I plan to graduate from the University of Missouri in May 2023.
